About AFX Studio

Founded in 2025 by Asad Khan and Alvina Khan, AFX Studio is Hyderabad’s first all-in-one creative studio designed to meet every creative requirement of businesses, brands, content creators, and events. From cinematic video editing, motion graphics, VFX, 2D & 3D animations, branding, digital solutions to website design — AFX Studio provides a structured, professional, and future-ready workflow to deliver premium output for every client. The studio brings together a team of 30+ experts who focus on creativity, precision, and innovation across all projects. Whether it’s producing a high-quality social media reel, a corporate video, an ad film, or a wedding cinematic edit, AFX Studio ensures every deliverable is on-brand, visually stunning, and professionally polished. We serve clients locally in Hyderabad and worldwide with remote editing solutions, making your project scalable and consistent across all platforms.
